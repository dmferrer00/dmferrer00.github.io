Project Overview

Purpose

This website will display the excellent work Big Papi that users can choose to purchase or just view, it will also contain some information about the artist, paintings, as well as contact info.

Intend Audience

This website is intended for anyone who is interested in purchasing art from Big Papi

Overview of content

The first page, the home page will include a picture of the artist, a short description of the artist, and their name. The next page, the gallery, will include the paintings of Big Papi, along with links to purchase them. The next page, the contact page, will include the artist's contact information. Another page will be the How It's Made page, which will include some background information on the artists inspiration and why they chose the style of art they did. The final page will be a payment page that users will be able to use to purchase the paintings.

Dynamic Functionality

As far as the Java Script Functionality goes it wont really be much, I will have some quality of life features. The hyperlinks will always show on the page, I will also make some of the images zoom in on when you hover over them. I want to add a cart feature, where users can add items to their cart and checkout, that will display how many items are in the cart on all pages. .
