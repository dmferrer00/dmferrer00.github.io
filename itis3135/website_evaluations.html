Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It designs, develops, and launches personal computers, tablets, wearables, and services.

  • Contrast: Apple uses high contrast between product images and simple backgrounds, making products the clear focal point, like on the iPhone or MacBook pages​
  • Repetition: The navigation bar, fonts, and color palette are consistently used across all pages, reinforcing a cohesive brand identity​
  • Alignment: Apple’s website adheres to clean grid-based layouts, aligning text and images precisely to maintain a structured and organized look​
  • Proximity: Related elements, such as product descriptions and purchase options, are grouped together, with ample whitespace separating unrelated sections for clarity

Craigslist
  • Contrast: Craigslist uses minimal visual contrast, relying on basic black text on a white background and blue links, which keeps the design functional but plain.
  • Repetition: The layout and font choices remain consistent across the entire site, with uniform lists and link styles providing a familiar user experience.
  • Alignment: Text and listings are left-aligned, giving the site a straightforward, no-frills structure that is easy to follow.
  • Proximity: Items within each category are grouped closely together, but the site's simple layout sometimes makes it difficult to distinguish between sections quickly.
